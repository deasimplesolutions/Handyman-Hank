About Hank's Handyman

DFW's Premier Home Services Partner Since 2019

Hank's Handyman was founded in 2019 with a simple mission: to eliminate the frustration that homeowners and businesses experience when dealing with contractors. We believe that quality workmanship, reliability, and genuine customer care shouldn't be luxuries—they should be the standard.

As a family-owned business serving the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, we've earned our reputation as DFW's #1 rated handyman service. Every project we undertake reflects our core values: exceptional craftsmanship, unwavering reliability, transparent communication, and comprehensive warranties that stand behind our work.

Our journey is rooted in purpose. We built Hank's Handyman to be a company where customer service isn't just a tagline—it's woven into everything we do. From the moment you call until long after we leave your property, we're committed to exceeding your expectations and making your life easier.
